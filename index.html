How We Approach Innovation

With over a decade of experience serving as a Chief Innovation Officer, it may seem that technology is always the primary driver of improvement. In practice, however, technology is only one part of the solution—not the strategy itself.

Effective innovation begins with a clear understanding of the underlying problem. Many of the most impactful improvements come from non-technical changes—addressing behaviors, refining processes, and challenging the mindset of “this is the way we have always done it.”

Organizational complacency is often the greatest barrier to progress. Identifying inefficiencies at their source allows for more thoughtful and effective solutions.

Only after those opportunities are clearly understood does technology become part of the solution—applied in a way that supports real needs rather than driving unnecessary complexity.

Using this approach, Leedham and Associates has consistently delivered practical, strategic, and technology-enabled improvements that produce measurable results.
